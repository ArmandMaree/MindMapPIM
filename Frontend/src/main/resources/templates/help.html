Movement

Click and drag on any bubble to move it. Move the whole Bubble Map by dragging the background. You can also zoom in and out by scrolling the mouse wheel.

Expanding

Right click on any bubble that you want to expand and select "Expand Bubble". This will pop up more topics relevant to you.

Delete

Right click on any bubble that you want to delete and select "Delete Bubble".

More Information

Double click on a bubble to show you more information about that bubble. This includes emails and facebook posts related to that bubble.
